About Me

I am currently a second year Ph.D. student in Department of Statistics and Data Science at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU), advised by Kathryn Roeder and Aaditya Ramdas. Before coming to CMU, I obtained a Bacholor degree in statistics from the Special Class for the Gifted Young (SCGY), University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2018, where I worked with Zemin Zheng in high-dimensional statistical inference.

Research Interests

I am interested in developing statistical and machine learning methodology and theory for sequential, high dimensional data analysis with statistical gaurantees. Some of my current research interests include the following

  • Online multiple testing
  • Statistical genomics
  • High-dimensinal graphical structure recovery
